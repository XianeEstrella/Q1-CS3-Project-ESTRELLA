What is Addition?

Addition means summing up two or more numbers or values, resulting to another number. For example, when we add 2 and 3 we get 5.

2 + 3 = 5

Suppose we have 4 mangoes in a basket. Then, upon harvesting another 2 mangoes, how many mangoes do we have now?

4 → 1 + 1 + 1 + 1

2 → 1 + 1

4 + 2 = 1 + 1 + 1 + 1 + 1 + 1

Now, if we count the mangoes, it is equal to 6.

CONCLUSION: THE PROPERTY OF ADDITION IS TO INCREASE THE VALUE BY ADDING ANOTHER VALUE TO IT.
